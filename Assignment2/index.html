What's new at Rocky north campus?

To live on campus while doing your studies at CQ University is a great opportunity to meet new friends and have a lot of fun! There are a lot of activities during the term and we can garanti that you will have a great stay.

Brampton Island

Surrounded by golden sands and sparkling blue waters, Brampton Island is the perfect location for a romantic getaway. Explore walking tracks leading to magnificent views, or snorkel among brightly coloured fish in the surrounding reefs.

Broken River Mountain Retreat

Located in Eungella National Park, Broken River Mountain Retreat is a haven for nature lovers. Picture yourself canoeing in a fresh mountain stream, swimming with a platypus or walking through ancient rainforests.

Cape Hillsborough National Park

Broad sandy beaches and rocky headlands formed by early volcanic activity make Cape Hillsborough one of the most scenic National Parks along the Central Queensland coast. This area is well known for the inquisitive wallabies that come down to the palm-fringed beach each morning at sunrise.
